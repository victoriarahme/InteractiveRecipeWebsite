Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine all dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Gradually add boiling water and stir until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.
